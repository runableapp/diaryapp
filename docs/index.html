A beautifully simple, secure personal journal. Write freely, attach photos, and keep your memories safe with password protection and AES-256 encryption.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Diary keeps things simple: a calendar, a rich text editor, and strong encryption. No cloud. No accounts. Just your words, on your machine.

🔒

Password Protected

Lock your diary with a master password. Auto-lock kicks in after a configurable period of inactivity.

🗓

Calendar Navigation

Browse entries by date with an intuitive monthly calendar. Dots mark days with entries so you never lose track.

✏

Rich Text Editor

Bold, italic, lists, headings, colors, and more. Write expressively with a full formatting toolbar.

📷

Photo Attachments

Embed images directly in your entries. Attach multiple photos to capture every moment of your day.

🔍

Full-Text Search

Instantly search across all entries by content, title, or date. Find any memory in seconds.

🌐

Multilingual

Supports 14 languages including English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, French, German, and more. Dates adapt to your locale.

🛡

AES-256 Encryption

Your entries are encrypted with AES-256. Data stays local — no cloud, no telemetry, no tracking.

🎨

Light & Dark Themes

Choose the look that suits you. Switch between light and dark themes from Settings.

💻

Cross-Platform

Runs natively on Linux, Windows, and macOS. Same experience everywhere, built with Flutter.

Screenshots

See it in action

A quick tour of the app: from the lock screen to the editor, search, and settings.

Secure Lock Screen

Your diary greets you with a clean lock screen. Enter your master password to access your entries. The app auto-locks after inactivity so your thoughts stay private.

Write Your Day

Select a date on the calendar and start writing. The rich text editor supports formatting, headings, lists, and more. Content auto-saves as you type.

Rich Formatting

Express yourself with bold headings, colored text, numbered lists, and bullet points. The full toolbar puts every formatting option at your fingertips.

Photo Memories

Attach as many photos as you like. Images are embedded directly in the entry so your visual memories live alongside your words.

Search & Browse

Search across all entries by keyword, title, or date. The list view shows every entry at a glance with date, title, and a content preview.

Settings & Security

Configure your password, lock timeout, data directory, and theme. Everything is designed to be straightforward and accessible.

Safe Deletion

Deleting an entry requires explicit confirmation. No accidental deletions — your memories are protected from mishaps.
